Key Principles of Material Design

layers

Material is the metaphor

The material is grounded in tactile reality, inspired by the study of paper and ink.

format_bold

Bold, graphic, intentional

Elements and components like typography, grids, space, scale, color, and imagery create hierarchy.

motion_photos_on

Motion provides meaning

Motion respects and reinforces the user as the prime mover, with transformations as key interactions.
